## 2025-05-24 - Accessibility improvements for icon-only buttons\n**Learning:** Icon-only buttons and expandable components often lack the required `aria-label`, `aria-expanded` and `aria-controls` attributes, which creates discoverability and contextual issues for screen-reader users.\n**Action:** Ensure all icon-only buttons include descriptive `aria-label`s, and expandable toggle sections explicitly utilize `aria-expanded` and `aria-controls` to link the toggle and content appropriately.
